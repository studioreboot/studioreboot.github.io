This game requires two players in order for the game
to function.

This is a regular game of snake, adjusted to accommodate for two players.

Once you tap this screen, you will be required to get the highest score
within ONE minute. Once the timer in the middle of the screen reaches 0
the person with the highest score wins.

Your score is indicated by the the number in the middle of the gameboard.
There are two, a partner and yourself will have to figure out who goes
to which side.

There IS music that is disabled by default.
To enable it, add "?playMusic=true" to the end of the URL.

[tap the screen to play]
